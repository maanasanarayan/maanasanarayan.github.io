inspiration: |
  I was reading **Tove Jansson's "The Summer Book"** on a flight back from
  Helsinki, and there's a passage where the grandmother describes the
  cottage rooms as overlapping rectangles she can walk between in her
  head. I drew the shapes on the airplane napkin. The finished collage
  is the napkin, three months later, with the angles fixed.
