description: 'Cut-paper collage on heavy cotton stock, mounted under glass.'
inspiration: |
  I was reading **Tove Jansson's "The Summer Book"** on a flight back from
  Helsinki and there's a passage where the grandmother describes the
  cottage rooms as overlapping rectangles she can walk between in her
  head. I drew the shapes on the airplane napkin. This collage is the
  finished version, three months later, with the angles fixed.

The paper is **Canson Mi-Teintes** 160 gsm, cut with a scalpel against a
steel rule. The dashed line at the bottom is a single piece of
gum-strip torn precisely along the perforation.
